Description of the additional special tiles:

(1) OneMoreOrder:

When the action of the player activates this special tile, it will give this player 

another chance of making action in this round. So the player will have another chance

of exchanging tiles, placing word or setting special tile.


(2) eliminateMultipler:

When the action of the player activates the special tile, its effect will eliminate

all the effects of the Multipler of the locations of the new added tiles. So the scores

player will get from this action may decrease.
